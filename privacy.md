# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 21, 2026**

WearShot ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by Markus Hammerschmid. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the WearShot app ("App").

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Photos and Images
When you use the virtual try-on feature, you provide photos from your camera or photo library. These photos may contain your face or the faces of other people.

**Important — Face Data:** WearShot does not perform facial recognition, identity verification, biometric identification, face tracking, or emotion detection. Faces in your photos are incidentally present because you upload personal photos. We do not extract biometric identifiers from any image. The visual appearance of a face may be preserved in the AI-generated output because the goal of the feature is to show you wearing an outfit — not to analyze or identify you.

Photos are transmitted to our AI processing service solely to generate the requested outfit visualization result. We do not store, retain, or share your photos beyond what is necessary to fulfill your request.

### Usage Data
We collect limited, anonymized usage data to improve the App, including generation counts and feature interactions. This data is stored locally on your device.

### Purchase Information
If you subscribe to WearShot Premium, purchases are processed through the Apple App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat may collect your App Store receipt and subscription state. We do not have access to your payment details.  
See RevenueCat's privacy policy: [https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)

### Name (Optional)
During onboarding, you may enter your first name. This is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To provide the AI virtual try-on feature — generating outfit visualizations on your photos
- To preserve the visual appearance of the subject in the generated output
- To manage your subscription status
- To personalize your in-app experience
- To improve the App's performance and features

---

## 3. Third-Party AI Processing

Uploaded images are securely transmitted to **Google Gemini API** (a service provided by Google LLC) solely for the purpose of generating outfit visualization results. Google processes these images in accordance with its own privacy policy and usage policies.

- Images are transmitted over encrypted connections.
- Google does not receive any identifying information about you beyond the image content itself.
- Your images are not used by us for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than generating the requested output.
- We do not sell your data to any third party.

See Google's privacy policy: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

We also share data with:
- **RevenueCat** — to manage in-app subscriptions.
- **Apple** — as required for App Store purchases and analytics.

---

## 4. Data Retention

Photos you submit for try-on are processed in real time and are not stored on our servers after the result is returned. Generated images are saved locally on your device only, unless you explicitly share them.

We may retain minimal technical metadata (not images) for a limited period to support abuse prevention and service integrity.

---

## 5. Your Rights

You may:
- Delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the App
- Manage or cancel your subscription via your Apple ID settings
- Contact us to request information about your data or to request deletion of any data we may hold

---

## 6. Allowed Usage

You may only upload photos of yourself or images for which you have explicit permission. Do not upload photos of other people without their consent. WearShot is intended solely for personal outfit and styling visualization.

---

## 7. Security

We implement reasonable technical safeguards to protect your data during transmission and processing. Images are sent over encrypted connections. We work with service providers that maintain appropriate security standards.

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

WearShot is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
